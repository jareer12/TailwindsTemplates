Clear and organized discussions

With a clear hierarchy of channels, threads and comments Playgroup allows members to navigate between discussions with ease. Because that is often not enough as a forum grows, members can also mark threads they want to come back to as Follow-ups.

Extendable member profiles

Because the most important part of your community are your members, Playgroup let's you a search members by interest. The detailed profiles can be customized with fields of your choice.

Moving forums made easy

IMPORT EXISTING CONTENT AND USERS FROM OTHER FORUMS

Security

If you're already running a forum, bring over your existing users and content in minutes. Nothing is lost in the process and existing users can simply sign up again, keeping their content connected to their new account. Currently only available for Discourse.
